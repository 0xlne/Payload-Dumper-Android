<p><b>Payload dumper</b> extracts boot.img and other partition images (boot, vendor_boot, etc.) from a payload.bin or ota.zip directly on your Android device.</p>

  - Real-time progress bar for extraction status
  - Integrity check with hash verification for extracted images
  - Multi-architecture support for broader device compatibility
  - Direct ota zip extraction without manual unpacking
  - Raw data view to inspect extracted content
  - Debug logging for troubleshooting and diagnostics
  - Incremental ota detection (extraction of incremental otas is not supported yet)


<b>Upcoming features:</b>

  - ability to cancel an ongoing extraction
  - option to select from/to USB storage
